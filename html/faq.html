FAQs

What is SEC?

SEC is an interactive cell to help students step out of their comfort zone and perform things they think are not their cup of tea. The cell aims to empower students and keep them self-motivated, self-determined by focusing on soft skills, communication skills, personal growth, team work and analyse and understand their issues.

Why SEC?

Having the process to follow is good but executing it turns the concept into reality. SEC provides the platform to practice the skills and enhance the abilities, recognize their strengths and practice them regularly as well as focus and conquer their weaknesses.
How SEC works?

To build anything we require a plan for development. SEC aims to work with each and every individual to lead them to a confident tomorrow by shaping their personality, communication skills and organizing their strengths in a positive direction today. SEC will first conduct the sessions based on these skills, identify its ratio according to particular individual and then focus on particular skill as per the analysis done.
What kind of activities are performed in development of the student?
  • Self-analysis based on analysis matrix.
  • Working on the desired goals based on analysis report.
  • Provide a suitable platform through activities and events.
  • Event-wise analysis report of each member.
  • Regular interaction with the team.
Which parts of personality development does SEC focus on?
  • Communication skills

  • Interpersonal skills

  • Intrapersonal skills

  • Creativity

  • Leadership skills

Who can join SEC?

Any student whose prime focus is development of the skills essential to sustain in professional and competitive environment.
How can on enroll in the community?

Visit the website secviit and navigate to the members login and signup page, fill in the essential details and you are ready to go.
Are there any prerequisites to enroll?

The only prerequisite is willingness to learn and persistence to practice.
Is it compulsory to enroll in all the skills?

The choice is yours, but if one chooses a particular domain one ought to complete all the activities associated with it.
How much time will one require to master the skills?

Time to complete totally depends on the number of courses enrolled and the dedication you have towards not only completing but also practicing them.
Why should I opt for a community instead of taking the course online?

Being a part of the community focuses on the overall development of individual and helps have one to one interaction. The factor of analysis report segregates the factors most essential and suggests the topics on which emphasis should be laid on.
